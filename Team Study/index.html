About Team Study

Team Study is a web platform structured in groups.

Team Study is a structured web application for managing groups of classes in secondary and primary levels for schools and private institutes or academies management support.

The groups are made up of students and the teacher or teachers who will serve as administrators group. The members of each group will have at their disposal five main tools are:

  • Wiki
  • Document Repository
  • Forum
  • Chat
  • Control Panel

Groups can be associated subgroups

In this way makes it easy for teachers manage work in groups, for example being able to use the repository of documents for deliveries of work, and students providing basic when managing a project tools, such as a forum, and initiating them into simple management tools real projects.

Mission

The company mission is to promote the study team.
Simplicity is the main idea of the platform, we do not want a platform that has to deliver with an instruction book, but a platform in which the teacher from and see at a glance as used, and this is achieved with an interface simple, and above using tools that are already familiar as they are forums or wikis.

Vision

We want to change the current study system through the platform Team Study.
